Fantasy Basketball Calculator
Welcome to Fantasy Basketball Calculator! The goal is to provide accurate fantasy basketball draft projections through mathematical analysis. Currently, the application only supports scoring for points leagues, with goals of incorporating category leagues in the future. To generate predictions, the system functions on a few fundamental concepts:

1. Player output calculated from per-game averages and total season fantasy points.
2. Average year-to-year progressions of players collected over the last 20 years.
3. Player correlations: using close player matches to predict next year's output.
4. Average next year score based on players who had similar output at the given player's age.

** Important note: since there is no NBA data available for incoming rookies, they are not included in the predictions. **
All stats were collected from Basketball Reference
