Fantasy Basketball Calculator
Welcome to Fantasy Basketball Calculator! Our goal is to provide accurate fantasy basketball draft projections through mathematical analysis. Currently, the application only supports scoring for points leagues, with goals of incorporating category leagues in the future. To generate predictions, the system functions on a few fundamental concepts:

1. Player output calculated from both per-game averages and total season fantasy points. The best ability is availability!
2. Average year-to-year progressions of player output collected over the last 20 years.
3. Player progress correlations: matching players' progress and using close matches to predict next year's output (used more heavily for players with more years in the league).
4. Average next year score based on players who had similar output at the given player's age (used more heavily for players with fewer years in the league).

Important note: since there is no NBA data available for incoming rookies, they are not included in the predictions.

All stats were collected from Basketball Reference
